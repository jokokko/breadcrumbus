# Privacy Policy

Breadcrumbus does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Permissions

| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| `tabs` | Read the active tab URL to generate breadcrumbs |
| `storage` | Save your display preferences locally on your device |
| `cookies` | Open links in the correct Firefox container (Firefox only) |

No data ever leaves your browser. The extension makes no network requests.
